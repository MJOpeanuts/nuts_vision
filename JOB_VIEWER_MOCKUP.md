## Color Scheme

- **Success (MPN extracted)**: Green background (#d4edda)
- **Warning (MPN not extracted)**: Yellow/orange background (#fff3cd)
- **Error (Database not connected)**: Red background (#f8d7da)
- **Info (No data available)**: Blue background (#d1ecf1)
- **Primary Color**: Blue (#1f77b4)
- **Text**: Dark gray (#2c3e50)

## Interactions

1. **Dropdown Selection**: Click to open, select job to view
2. **Expandable Sections**: Click arrow to expand/collapse IC details
3. **Images**: Display at full width within their container
4. **Scrolling**: Vertical scroll to navigate through all components

## Responsive Behavior

- On smaller screens, the two-column layout may stack vertically
- Images scale to fit container width
- Text wraps appropriately
- Metrics boxes adapt to screen size
